## 5. App Details

**2,800 character limit (100 minimum) — Markdown supported**

```markdown
Prowl monitors your product pages every day and alerts you the moment something breaks. A missing Add to Cart button, broken variant selectors, images that won't load, or a price that doesn't display — these silent issues stop customers from buying without triggering any error on your end. Prowl catches them before your customers notice.

**How it works**

Add your product pages and Prowl handles the rest. Every day, Prowl visits each page in a real browser — just like a customer would. It checks that images load, prices display, and variant selectors work. Then it runs a full purchase flow test: selecting a variant, clicking Add to Cart, and verifying the item actually reaches the cart.

When an issue is found, AI analysis reviews the page, confirms the problem is real, and generates a clear explanation of what went wrong. You receive an email alert with a screenshot of the page, a plain-language description of the issue, and a specific suggested fix you can act on right away.

**What Prowl detects**

Prowl monitors for the product page issues that directly block purchases:

- Add to Cart buttons that are missing, hidden, or non-functional
- Variant selectors that don't respond (size, color, style)
- Product images that fail to load
- Missing or hidden prices
- JavaScript errors affecting page functionality
- Theme rendering issues
- Slow page load times

**Smart alerting, not alert spam**

High-confidence issues trigger an immediate alert — no waiting for a second scan. Lower-confidence findings are re-checked before alerting to avoid false alarms. You can acknowledge any alert to stop repeat notifications, and issues resolve automatically when Prowl confirms they're fixed.

Prowl works with every Shopify theme. No code changes, no configuration, no developer needed. Just add your pages and start monitoring.
```

_(1,539 characters)_

## 16. Testing Instructions

**For Shopify app review team**

```
TESTING INSTRUCTIONS FOR PROWL

Prowl monitors product pages for broken functionality and alerts merchants.
Here's how to test the complete flow:

SETUP
1. Install the app. You will see the onboarding flow.
2. Click "Choose plan" to activate the $10/month plan (14-day free trial).
3. After billing is confirmed, you'll land on the dashboard.

ADD PRODUCT PAGES
4. Click "Add product page" from the dashboard or the Product Pages nav item.
5. Select 1–3 products from your store's product catalog.
6. Each product page will show a "Pending" status.

TRIGGER A SCAN
7. Click the "Scan now" button on any product page to trigger an immediate scan.
8. The scan takes approximately 45–60 seconds. The page will show "Running" status.
9. When complete, the status will update to Healthy, Warning, or Critical.

REVIEW RESULTS
10. Click on a product page to view its scan history and detected issues.
11. If issues were found, click an issue to see:
    - The screenshot of the page at scan time
    - AI-generated explanation of the problem
    - Suggested fix
    - Severity level and confidence score
12. Click "Acknowledge" on any issue to mark it as reviewed (stops alert emails).

ALERTS
13. Alerts are sent via email for high-severity issues.
14. The alert email includes an inline screenshot, explanation, and suggested fix.
15. Alert emails go to the shop owner email (or custom alert email in Settings).

SETTINGS
16. Navigate to Settings to configure:
    - Custom alert email address
    - Enable/disable email alerts
    - Enable/disable Shopify admin notifications

NOTES
- Scheduled scans run daily at 6:00 AM UTC automatically.
- Deep scans (with full purchase flow testing) run on first scan,
  when critical issues are open, and weekly on Mondays.
- The app requests only the "read_products" Shopify API scope.
- No customer data is accessed. Scans run as a public visitor.
- Screenshots are stored privately and only visible to the authenticated merchant.
```
